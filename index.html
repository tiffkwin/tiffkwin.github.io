ABOUT ME

Small kid, big dreams.

I'm the girl who fell in love at first "Hello World". I am currently an undergraduate student at East Carolina University pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer science with minors in neuroscience and mathematics. My interests include the applications of technology in healthcare, machine learning, computer science education, and bringing opportunities in technology to underrepresented communities. I'm at the start of a long journey with a short to-do list. "Gain experience, share my passions, and change the world," is the gist of it.

Welcome to my website. Here you can follow my journey and see what I've been up to. Talk to me about anything involving the intersection of technology and healthcare, community outreach and involvement, the amazing feeling you get from watching younger students fall in love with tech, and my obsession with making Spotify playlists (200 and counting!).

I'm a proud member of the EC Scholar family at ECU, a scholarship program that has brought me more opportunities than I could have ever dreamt of. Through the program, I have found a place in ECU that urges me to work towards the improvement of our shared community and to help encourage the unity of our diverse minds, beliefs, and experiences. I found a distinct sense of pride, tinted purple and gold, that persists through the good and bad times, reminding me that I am not alone nor will I ever be as long as I'm a Pirate.
